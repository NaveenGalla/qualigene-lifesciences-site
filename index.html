About Qualigene

A successful and research-driven company with a future-ready vision.

Who we are

Qualigene Lifesciences is a startup led by qualified professionals with experience across pharmaceutical research and development, veterinary products, and aquaculture product development.

Our long-term direction is clear: replace conventional antibiotic dependence with smarter microbial and bacteriophage-led solutions that support performance, export quality, and healthier environments.

Why teams choose us

  • Innovative, cost-conscious formulations shaped by on-ground farm realities.
  • Continuous process upgrades focused on quality, consistency, and scalability.
  • Cross-functional expertise spanning manufacturing, quality control, and R&D.
  • Focused development pipeline for poultry, aquaculture, and future human health programs.
