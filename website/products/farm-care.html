Product overview

Farm Care is a CAA-approved water and soil probiotic specifically formulated for shrimp and fish aquaculture systems. At a potency of not less than 20 billion CFU/gm, it introduces a high concentration of beneficial microbial organisms into pond environments — stabilising water quality, improving the gut health of cultured species, and reducing pathogen pressure through competitive exclusion.

Key benefits

  • Improve Pond Health — stabilises water quality and beneficial microbial populations
  • Boost Shrimp & Fish Immunity — strengthens the natural immune response of cultured species
  • Maintain pH & Microbial Balance — keeps water chemistry and microbial ratios in check
  • Potency: Not less than 20 billion CFU/gm
  • CAA document available for commercial and regulatory purposes
  • Manufactured at GMP · HACCP · ISO certified facility, Bengaluru
