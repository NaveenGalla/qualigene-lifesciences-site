Advanced Aqua Range Enterodragon

The EHP challenge

EHP (Enterocytozoon hepatopenaei) is a microsporidian parasite that colonises the hepatopancreas of shrimp, impairing digestion and energy conversion. EHP-positive ponds suffer from stunted growth, increased feed conversion ratios, and significantly reduced harvest weights — making it one of the most commercially costly conditions in modern shrimp farming.

Enterodragon is formulated as a feed-mixed supplement that works to support the microbial environment of the shrimp gut, improve energy extraction from feed, and reduce the performance gap caused by EHP in affected ponds.

Key features

  • Targets EHP-impacted shrimp culture as a feed supplement
  • Supports gut microbial health and hepatopancreas function
  • Improves energy conversion in EHP-positive shrimp populations
  • Can be used in prevention and active management phases
