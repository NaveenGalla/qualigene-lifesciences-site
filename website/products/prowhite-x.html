What ProWhite-X addresses

White gut syndrome is caused by excessive feeding on algae and cyanobacteria in the pond, leading to inflammation and discolouration of the shrimp gut. Left untreated, it reduces feed intake, impairs digestion, and significantly lowers harvest weights. In intensive operations, it can spread rapidly and affect the entire pond crop.

ProWhite-X uses a probiotic-led approach to rebalance the gut environment, reduce the microbial conditions that encourage white gut, and restore healthy gut function — improving shrimp performance and water quality simultaneously.

  • Promotes healthy shrimp culture — improves gut function and feed utilisation

White gut — commercial impact

White gut consistently causes 10–30% losses in affected ponds. ProWhite-X targets the condition at its root — preventing the probiotic imbalance that allows it to establish.

Dual outcome

ProWhite-X improves both shrimp gut health and pond water quality — addressing the condition from two angles simultaneously for faster and more durable results.
