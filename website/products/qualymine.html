Why mineral supplementation matters

Shrimp require a consistent supply of dissolved minerals for successful moulting, shell formation, and immune function. Mineral depletion in pond water — especially during intensive culture cycles — directly impacts survival rates, growth performance, and the quality of the final harvest. Qualymine delivers a concentrated liquid mineral dose directly into the water column to address this gap throughout the cycle.
