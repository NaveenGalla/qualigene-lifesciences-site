What V-Solve does

Vibrio bacteria are among the most commercially damaging pathogens in shrimp aquaculture — responsible for luminescent disease, early mortality syndrome, and significant batch losses. V-Solve targets Vibrio colonies directly, reducing their population in pond water to protect shrimp health and improve survival across grow-out cycles.
