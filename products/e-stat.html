The case for poultry probiotics

Conventional poultry production has long depended on routine antibiotic use for disease prevention and growth promotion. Increasing regulatory pressure, consumer demand, and export market requirements are accelerating a shift toward probiotic-led alternatives that deliver measurable health outcomes without the long-term risks of antibiotic dependency.

E-STAT supports this transition — providing a research-led microbial formulation that competes against harmful gut bacteria, supports immune development in young birds, and improves overall flock performance with a cleaner product profile.

Key benefits

  • Improves microbial balance in the gut and farm environment
  • Reduces colonisation of harmful bacteria (e.g. Salmonella, Clostridium)
  • Supports healthier growth rates and better feed conversion
  • Helps reduce reliance on routine antibiotic use
  • Suitable for broiler and layer operations at commercial scale
  • Manufactured at GMP · HACCP · ISO certified facility, Bengaluru

Research-led formulation

Developed by a team with pharmaceutical and veterinary expertise — E-STAT reflects Qualigene's commitment to science-backed alternatives to antibiotic dependency.

Supports export readiness

Antibiotic-free or antibiotic-reduced flock programs are increasingly required for export markets — E-STAT helps farms move in that direction.

Company-level certification

GMP, HACCP, and ISO certifications at company level provide the trust foundation that commercial poultry buyers and distributors need.
